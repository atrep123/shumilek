# Pixel Art Tips

Creating pixel art is like mining — you chip away one pixel at a time until the gem reveals itself.

## Color Palette Rules

1. **Limit your palette** — 8-16 colors max
2. **Use hue shifting** — shadows aren't just darker, they shift to cooler hues
3. **Dithering** — checkerboard patterns for smooth gradients
4. **Outlines** — dark outlines make sprites pop against any background

## Dark Theme Colors

For a Stardew-like dark aesthetic:
- Background: `#0D0B1A` (void black)
- Panels: `#1E1A2E` (deep purple)
- Accent: `#D4A017` (lantern gold)
- Text: `#C8B8E8` (moonlight lavender)
- Links: `#40E0D0` (crystal cyan)
