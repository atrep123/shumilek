# Bestiary

Creatures of the deep mines, catalogued by Shumilek.

## Slimes

Green, blue, red, purple — each more dangerous than the last. The purple ones dwell in the deepest shadows.

- **Green Slime**: Levels 1-20, harmless
- **Blue Slime**: Levels 41-60, freezing touch
- **Red Slime**: Levels 81-100, burns on contact
- **Purple Slime**: Levels 100+, shadow magic

## Shadow Brutes

Large, dark creatures that phase through walls. They guard the ancient [[Minerals]] deposits.

## Serpents

Flying creatures of the skull cavern. Fast and deadly.
