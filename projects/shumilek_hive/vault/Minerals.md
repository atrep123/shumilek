# Minerals

The crystals of the deep mines, each with unique properties.

## Common Minerals

| Mineral | Color | Depth | Value |
|---------|-------|-------|-------|
| Amethyst | Purple | 1-40 | 100g |
| Aquamarine | Blue | 41-80 | 180g |
| Ruby | Red | 81-120 | 250g |
| Diamond | Clear | 50+ | 750g |

## Rare Crystals

- **Prismatic Shard**: Rainbow crystal, extremely rare. Used for the galaxy sword.
- **Void Essence**: Drops from shadow creatures. See [[Bestiary]]
- **Solar Essence**: Glows with inner light.

## Uses

Minerals can be donated to the museum, gifted to villagers, or used in crafting.
