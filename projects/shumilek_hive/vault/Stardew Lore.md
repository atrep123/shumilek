# Stardew Lore

The valley holds many secrets. Deep in the mines, ancient dwarven texts speak of a knowledge keeper — **Shumilek** — the archivist who catalogued every crystal, every shadow.

## The Mines

The deeper you go, the darker it gets. But the crystals glow brighter. 

- Level 1-40: Earth and stone
- Level 41-80: Frozen depths  
- Level 81-120: Lava and shadow
