# Welcome to Shumilek Notes

Welcome, traveler. You've found the **Dark Archive** — a place where knowledge lives in the shadows.

## Getting Started

- Create new notes with **Ctrl+N**
- Link notes together with `[[wiki-links]]` like [[Daily Log]]
- Search your vault with **Ctrl+F**
- Toggle the graph view with **Ctrl+G**
- Save with **Ctrl+S**

## Features

This is your personal knowledge base, inspired by the ancient archives.

- **Wiki-links**: Connect your thoughts with `[[Note Name]]`
- **Tags**: Organize with #tags like #welcome #getting-started
- **Graph View**: See how all your notes connect
- **Dark Theme**: Easy on the eyes, deep in the shadows

> "Knowledge is a lantern in the dark mine. The more you connect, the brighter it shines."
